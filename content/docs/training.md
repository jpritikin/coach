### Psilomethoxin 🐸

Psilomethoxin (Pm) is the ideal complement to psychotherapy.

- Better safety profile than Ketamine or MDMA; Pm can be used daily without medical supervision.
- Pm is not a scheduled substance, requiring no medical prescriber or DEA license.
- Pm offers enhanced emotional clarity, blunting of anxiety, and reduced interest in multitasking.
- Pm has a wide [psycholytic](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Psychedelic_therapy#Psycholytic_therapy) range.

The Church of Sacred Synthesis has [announced](https://thesacredsynthesis.com/church-of-the-sacred-synthesis-march-7-press-release/) that Pm will soon be available for sale over the counter (OTC) in a GMP formulation, enabling your clients to easily obtain the substance whether receiving in-person or telehealth counseling.

### Contraindications 🚫

Since Pm is similar to 5-MeO-DMT, it is prudent to regard
contraindications for 5-MeO-DMT as contraindications for Pm
until we have more data.

- Allergy to Psilocybe mushrooms[^mushroom]
- Epilepsy (a strong contraindication for 5-MeO-DMT)
- 5-MeO-DMT interacts with CYP2D6 and monoamine oxidase (MAO) inhibitors.
  - CYP2D6 inhibitors such as Paroxetine, Bupropion, Quinidine, etc
  - MAO inhibitors such as beta carbolines, harmala alkaloids, moclobemide, etc
- Serotonin reuptake inhibitors
- Lithium
- Pregnancy or breastfeeding

For more information, see:
- [F.I.V.E.'s Risks and Cautions](https://five-meo.education/risks-and-cautions/)
